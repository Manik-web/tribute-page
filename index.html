Georges St Pierre

Best Welterweight Ever

Here is a timeline of GSP's life:

  • 1981 - Born in Quebec
  • 1988 - started learning Karate to deal with a school bully
  • 1997 - began learning Brazilian Jiu Jitsu and Boxing
  • 2002 - Jan - Made his pro mma debut again Ivan Menjivar
  • 2002 - June Became the UCC Welterweight champion
  • 2004 - Jan - Made his UFC debut
  • 2004 - Oct - competed for the UFC Welterweight championship and lost to Matt Hughes
  • 2006 - Nov - re-fought Matt Hughes and beat him in the second round by TKO to become the Welterweight Champion
  • 2007 - April - Lost the belt in his first defense to Matt Serra in a shock upset
  • 2008 - April - re-gained the chmpionship from Matt Serra
  • 2013 - Nov - Defended against Johnny Hendricks and subsequently retired
  • 2017 - Nov - Came out of retirement to move up a weight class and win the UFC middleweight championship, defeating Michael Bisping
  • 2017 - Dec - Retired again due to medical reasons
  • Now - rumours abound that GSP may fight lightweight champ Khabib Nurmagomedov. GSP's current record stands at 26 - 2
